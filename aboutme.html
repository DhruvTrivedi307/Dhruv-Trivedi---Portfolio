I'm Dhruv, a programmer with a deep-rooted passion for both technology and creativity. My journey in the digital world started with a fascination for creating brands and products that resonate. Over the past two decades, I've had the privilege of bringing numerous ideas to life, obsessing over the details that make them successful.

In 2022, I decided to pivot my focus towards software development, driven by a desire to build solutions from the ground up. I’ve since immersed myself in coding, mastering languages like Java, JavaScript, C, Bash, HTML, and CSS. Each project I work on is a new opportunity to blend my creative background with my technical skills, striving to craft experiences that are as intuitive as they are innovative.

In addition to my coding journey, I run a creative studio called The Workshop, where I continue to explore the intersection of design, technology, and business. I’m also actively involved in investing in early-stage ventures, supporting ideas that have the potential to disrupt industries.

Outside of work, I'm an avid cricket player, movie enthusiast, console gamer, and traveller. I believe in living a balanced life, where work and play inspire each other.

Looking ahead, my vision is to become a leading programmer, contributing to impactful projects within a multinational company. I’m constantly learning, always curious, and excited about what the future holds in the ever-evolving tech landscape.
